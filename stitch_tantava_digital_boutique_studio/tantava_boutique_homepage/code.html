The Visionary

Nikitha Priya Bharana

"Tantava is not just a boutique; it's a revival of our roots. Every thread we weave tells a story of an artisan's soul, brought to life for the modern woman who values her heritage as much as her individuality."

Founded in Bengaluru, Tantava celebrates the confluence of time-honored Indian craftsmanship and contemporary fashion sensibilities. Nikitha's journey began with a passion for preserving the dying arts of hand-weaving and intricate Zardozi, reimagining them into silhouettes that resonate with global aesthetics.
